Product Details

Apple's 14-inch MacBook Pro has straddled the line between high-end consumer laptop and capable professional machine since its introduction, showing up as often in offices and media studios as it does in homes and dorm rooms. It's a true prosumer laptop, with options ranging from basic (the more affordable M3-powered model) to overpowered (the M3 Max monster). In the middle sits the 14-inch MacBook Pro with M3 Pro (starting at $1,599; $1,999 as tested).
